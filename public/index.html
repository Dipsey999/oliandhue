FAQ

Answered questions.

Everything you might want to know - up front.

1
What does Oli&Hue do?
Oli&Hue is a design and development studio. We handle product design (UI/UX), website design, branding, and software development — including AI-powered products and agentic AI integration.

For marketing, our sister brand Bumbee.Studio handles social media, paid ads, funnels, and analytics. Reach out to them directly at bumbee.studio.
2
What is Bumbee?
Bumbee.Studio is our sister marketing agency. They handle social media management, paid advertising, brand strategy, marketing funnels, landing pages, content marketing, and analytics. It's a separate brand — if you need marketing, reach out to them directly at bumbee.studio.
3
What is Agentic AI, and how do you use it?
Agentic AI refers to AI systems that can plan, execute, use tools, and complete tasks autonomously — not just answer questions. We build custom AI agents that integrate into your existing products and workflows, automate complex processes, and deliver real business outcomes. Think of it as giving your software a brain that acts, not just responds.
4
Do you handle marketing too, or just design and dev?
Oli&Hue focuses on design and software development. For marketing — social media, paid ads, funnels, and growth — our sister brand Bumbee.Studio handles that separately. You can work with us for design and dev, reach out to Bumbee for marketing, or both. They're connected but operate independently.
5
How does your AI-powered workflow actually work?
We use AI tools internally to accelerate our design and development work — from research to iteration to deployment. This means faster turnarounds, smarter insights, and more refined outputs. You get agency-quality work at startup speed because AI handles the heavy lifting while our team focuses on creativity and strategy.
6
Is there a contract or lock-in period?
No long-term lock-in. Our plans are flexible — you can pause, upgrade, downgrade, or cancel anytime with no penalty. We want you to stay because of the value, not because of a contract.
