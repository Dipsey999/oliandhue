Project Overview

HATCH is a next-gen social space.

Built for students, young professionals, and founders to connect, learn, and build together. Our goal was to craft a minimal, modern, and premium product experience that stands apart from traditional social platforms. We focused on creating an energetic yet professional interface that feels clean, intentional, and delightful to use, setting the foundation for Hatch to become the most refined social platform for the new generation of builders.
